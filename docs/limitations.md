# Limitations

- **Not a drug claim.** This repository demonstrates a **fast, explainable, patent-pending PDB-to-scaffold hypothesis workflow**. We do not claim a therapeutic product, clinical candidate, or validated medicine.
- **Workflow demonstration only.** Artifacts are software benchmark outputs for research, audit, and review—not experimental hit validation.
- **No wet-lab validation.** No binding, activity, stability, or safety data are included or implied.
- **No therapeutic claims.** Nothing in this repository should be interpreted as medical advice or clinical evidence.
- **Heuristic feature labels.** Pharmacophore types are inferred from residue context and may be wrong or incomplete.
- **False positives are possible.** Surface-exposed anchors may not correspond to functional hotspots.
- **Glycan-stripped viral examples are surface-mapping demos.** They are not immunogen-design packages and do not demonstrate immunogenicity.
- **Context translation is experimental.** Recorded temperature and pH metadata are not a full protonation/pKa simulation.
- **Public export is sanitized.** Local paths, engine internals, and generated molecules are intentionally removed.
